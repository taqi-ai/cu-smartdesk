# 🎓 CU SmartDesk: The Student Resolution Ecosystem
**Built for Hackophobia | Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash**

> "Attendance disappeared because of a scholarship glitch. The HOD says it's not their problem. Accounts says the system is locked. The student is left failing a subject they actually attended. This isn't a hypothetical—it is the daily reality for hundreds."

---

## 🛑 The Crisis: The "Bureaucratic Loop"
Students at **Chandigarh University UP Campus** currently face a fragmented administrative system where:
* **Scholarship Glitches:** Automated systems revoke scholarships without notice, causing attendance for those days to be marked as 'Absent', pushing students below the 75% threshold.
* **The Ghosting Effect:** Emails to departments go unanswered for weeks. Class mentors and HODs often lack the data or the authority to provide immediate fixes.
* **Zero Visibility:** Students have no way to calculate "Attendance Recovery" or "Safe Skip" margins, leading to unnecessary academic stress and debarment risks.
* **Fear of Reporting:** Quality issues (mess, labs, infrastructure) go unreported because students fear academic retaliation for criticizing the system.

---

## 💡 The Solution: SmartDesk
CU SmartDesk is a **Proactive Resolution Portal** that moves beyond simple complaint filing. It uses AI to bridge the gap between student rights and administrative action.

### ✨ Key Innovation Points:
* **📊 Proactive Attendance Analytics:** Don't just show a percentage. We tell you exactly how many classes you can skip safely and provide a mathematically calculated "Recovery Plan" to hit your 85% safety goal.
* **🕵️ AI-Moderated Anonymity:** A safe space for quality criticism. Our **Gemini-powered moderation engine** ensures feedback is constructive and abuse-free while keeping student identities 100% anonymous.
* **🚨 Smart Escalation Engine:** If an official doesn't respond within 48 hours, our AI automatically drafts a formal, firm, legal-grade letter (referencing the **RTI Act 2005**) addressed to the PVC/Registrar.
* **🔥 Campus Pulse:** We aggregate anonymous reports into a real-time trending dashboard. When 100+ students report a mess issue, it's no longer a "complaint"—it's a high-priority data point for the Registrar.
* **🛰️ CUIMS Sync:** A seamless simulation of real-time data fetching to provide a unified dashboard that the official UIMS currently lacks.

Our goal is to shift the university culture from **"Reactive Complaining"** to **"Data-Driven Resolution."** SmartDesk ensures that no student's academic record is ruined by a system error or an unanswered email.
